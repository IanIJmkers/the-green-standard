🎯 THE 4 WORKFLOWS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1️⃣  PRODUCT DROP AUTOMATION
    File: product-drop-automation.json
    
    What it does:
    • Listens for new product creation in Shopify
    • Uses AI (OpenAI) to write product descriptions & social captions
    • Sends to Telegram for team approval
    • Once approved: launches email, posts to socials, schedules content
    
    Best for: Product launches with instant content generation
    Integrations: Shopify, OpenAI, Telegram, Klaviyo, Buffer
    

2️⃣  CONTENT CALENDAR ENGINE
    File: content-calendar-engine.json
    
    What it does:
    • Runs at 9 AM CET every day
    • Pulls scheduled posts from your Notion content calendar
    • AI generates platform-optimized captions
    • Team reviews on Telegram before posting
    • Auto-posts to Buffer and updates Notion status
    
    Best for: Consistent daily content distribution
    Integrations: Notion, OpenAI, Telegram, Buffer
    

3️⃣  ABANDONED CART RECOVERY
    File: abandoned-cart-recovery.json
    
    What it does:
    • Watches for customers who leave items in cart
    • Sends 3 automated emails over 72 hours:
      - 1 hour: "Don't forget your items!"
      - 24 hours: Social proof email
      - 48 hours: 10% discount offer
    • Stops if customer completes purchase
    
    Best for: Recovering lost sales 24/7 automatically
    Integrations: Shopify, Klaviyo
    

4️⃣  WEEKLY ANALYTICS DIGEST
    File: weekly-analytics-digest.json
    
    What it does:
    • Runs every Monday at 8 AM CET
    • Collects data from 4 sources simultaneously:
      - Shopify orders & revenue (last 7 days)
      - Stripe payments (last 7 days)
      - Instagram metrics (impressions, reach, profile views)
      - TikTok analytics (views, followers)
    • Compiles into formatted Telegram report
    
    Best for: Weekly business performance overview
    Integrations: Shopify, Stripe, Instagram, TikTok, Telegram
