## Email 2: Care Guide (Sent 3 days after fulfilment)

**Subject:** how to look after your gear
**Preview:** a 2-minute care guide that makes everything last longer.

---

Your order should be arriving soon (or maybe it's already there).

Quick care guide so your gear lasts for years:

**Leather products (Carry Bag, Ball Pouch, Shoe Bag, Valuables Bag):**
- Wipe with a damp cloth after each round
- Apply leather conditioner every 2-3 months (any brand works)
- Store in the dust bag when not in use
- Don't worry about scratches — they add character

**Knit headcovers:**
- Machine wash cold, lay flat to dry
- Don't tumble dry — it'll shrink
- The knit loosens slightly with use — this is normal and helps it fit snugly

That's it. Simple gear, simple care.

Ian
