For: TikTok / Instagram Reels (brand awareness piece)

Voice: Your cloned voice — passionate but controlled, sincere

Somewhere along the way, golf became about who could afford to play.

Country club memberships. Thousand-euro bags. Dress codes that felt more like dress tests.

But the course itself? It doesn't care.

It doesn't care about your handicap. Your background. Your budget.

It just asks that you show up.

That's why we built The Green Standard.

Premium gear that doesn't gatekeep.

Because the course is for everyone.

And so are we.
